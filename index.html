Privacy Policy

Paintlet respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the app.

```

Information We Collect

Paintlet does not require users to create an account. The app does not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, videos, contacts, or precise location data.

Advertising

Paintlet uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising ID, app activity, diagnostics, and usage data for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement.

In-App Purchases

Paintlet uses Google Play Billing to process optional in-app purchases such as coin packs, boosters, and premium content. Payment information is processed by Google Play and is not stored by Paintlet.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The app may use Google/Firebase services for analytics, crash logs, diagnostics, and app performance monitoring.

Data Deletion

Paintlet does not provide user accounts. If you want to request deletion of data associated with the app, contact us at: uygulamadunyasi26@gmail.com

Children

Paintlet is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

Contact

Email: uygulamadunyasi26@gmail.com
```
